Who Am I?

Antonio Azzolino

A industrial designer and 3D artist with experience in developing scenography and three-dimensional projects. His work combines aesthetic planning with a practical understanding of essential factors such as lighting, textures, and the flow of people. With ease in translating concepts into viable projects, he has experience in prototyping with multiple materials to ensure a precise, functional delivery that is perfectly suited to the dynamics of a nightclub.
